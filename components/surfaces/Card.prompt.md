**Card** — the content container. White-on-cream with a hairline seat by default; `tone="bone"` for inset groups, `tone="dark"` for code/audit wells (dark inversion is the brand's emphasis move). Never fully-round a card.

```jsx
<Card>…</Card>
<Card tone="dark" pad={24}>…audit log…</Card>
<Card interactive>…hover-lifts…</Card>
```
