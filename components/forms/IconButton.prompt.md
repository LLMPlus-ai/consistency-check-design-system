**IconButton** — 36px circular icon-only control for toolbars, copy-to-clipboard, close, and carousel arrows. Always pass `ariaLabel`.

```jsx
<IconButton ariaLabel="Copy citation"><Icon name="copy" /></IconButton>
<IconButton variant="bare" ariaLabel="More"><Icon name="more-horizontal" /></IconButton>
```

Variants: `outline` (hairline) · `bare` (toolbar) · `dark`.
